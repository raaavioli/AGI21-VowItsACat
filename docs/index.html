The Project

Our project is a turn-based game, reminiscent of Auto Chess, designed for two players. The gameplay will loop between two different states, Draft Round and Combat Round, until either player has emerged victorious.

During the Draft Round, the players will interact with their respective sides of the board simultaneously, placing characters they have drafted to combat eachother. Once the players are done drafting and placing, the Combat Round starts.

During the Combat Round, the placed teams will automatically battle eachother in a random fashion, until either team is vanquished. Once a team has emerged victorious, it will then deal damage to the losing player. If a player takes too much damage, he/she will have lost, and the other player would win.

Tech-wise, the intent is to have the game run on a SUR40 device, using Microsoft PixelSense to detect objects added on the screen. What exactly those objects are is right now unclear, but it will be revealed along with the development, depending on what works and what doesn't.
