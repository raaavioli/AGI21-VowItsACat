The Team

Team Description

Oliver Eriksson

Graphics Programming, Visual Effects, Audio, Scripting

I have contributed to all parts of the project, however most of my work was done to give the game a visually pleasing look by writing shaders and creating particle effects. For example, I wrote shaders and created effects for most character projectiles, the card and character description UI, spawn platforms, team flags and the Squishy character. I created the environment model including procedural texturing and drew the skybox. I also mixed and added sound to the game. Here is my personal portfolio.

Samuel Westman Granlund

Interaction Programmer, Game Designer

Contributed to the design of the project, to the process of retrieving card data, and provided support in areas where possible. I made significant contributions to designing the gameplay loop. I also contributed to scripting, testing and debugging.

Anders Steen

Character Artist, Gameplay Programmer, Interaction Programmer

I created the character models, materials and animations in Blender, implemented Armor and Doctor as characters in the game, and made the initial computer vision and interaction programming with the SUR40.

Filip Berendt

Web Master, Game Designer and Programmer, UI Designer, Interaction Programmer

My primary responsibilities has been working on this development blog, handling contact and bookings, as well as designing the project concept. Responsibilities that I have shared with others includes mostly implementation of game, UI, and interaction elements. In general, I have had a hand in most activities by providing feedback, support, and discussion to my fellow team members.
